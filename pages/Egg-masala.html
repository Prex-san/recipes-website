Recipe for Egg Masala


What is Egg Masala?

Egg Masala is the most versatile side dish. It complements everything in existence.
Be it Naan, parotta, dosa, rice, or even bread. Egg masala has you covered!
The cherry on top is that it is easy to make! Just get the huge list of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Boiled eggs(ofc)
  • Spices
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Garlic Paste
    • Cumin Powder
    • Corriander Powder
    • Turmeric Powder
    • Red Chilli Powder
  • Tomato & Onion
  • Ginger & Garlic
  • Chilli & Curry Leaves
  • Water

Algorithm

To obtain the best Egg masala, follow these steps diligently
  1. Fry the boiled eggs first after adding all the spices.
  2. Now it's time to go full ROY MUSTANG ON THE PAN
  3. Get a pan and throw every single ingredient inside and in whatever proportion
    you feel like throwing em in.
  4. Keep going until you're satisfied.
  5. Add some water in the end to make it look like a gravy.
  6. Voila! It should work out. If not, call for delivery.
