How to make Parotta


What is parotta?

Parotta is one of the tastiest main dishes with an approprite side dish. The only problem is, parotta is extremely hard to make. In Tamil Nadu, a parotta stall is like McDonald's. It's good and handy but unhealthy. There's another similarity, in other countries there's a spot at a fast food firm for anyone who did not do well at school. Here, we have the almighty parotta stalls providing employment for the listless ones. No matter what's happening in the country there's always income in a parotta stall.
