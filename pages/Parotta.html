How to make Parotta


What is parotta?

Parotta is one of the tastiest main dishes with an approprite side dish. The only
problem is, parotta is extremely hard to make. In Tamil Nadu, a parotta stall is like
McDonald's. It's good and handy but unhealthy. There's another similarity, in other
countries there's a spot at a fast food firm for anyone who did not do well at school.
Here, we have the almighty parotta stalls providing employment for the listless ones.
No matter what's happening in the country there's always income in a parotta stall.

Ingredients

  • Maida
  • A lot of oil

Algorithm

To make a parotta, obtain a two year degree from a local parotta master
  1. The easiest way to enjoy this dish without having to go thorough heaven and hell
    is by buying it from a local parotta stall.
  2. Eat it with any side dish of your liking to get the complete experience. I'd suggest
    my exquisite egg masala.
