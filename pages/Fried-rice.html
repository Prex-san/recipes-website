Recipe for Fried Rice


What is Fried rice?

Unless you have been living under a rock for more than 2500 years, you must
have heard about fried rice. The long-standing rival or, as some might say, the
best ally of noodles is fried rice. It's easy to make and fun to eat. What more
do we need?

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Soya sauce
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Some veggies(optional)
    • Carrot
    • Beans
    • Green Onion
    • Cabbage

Algorithm

To obtain the best fried rice, follow these steps diligently
  1. Get a bowl and prepare to cut the vegetables.
  2. Cook the rice and refrigerate it for 4 hours.
    Put the sword aside and go watch some Netflix.
  3. After 4-6 hours get the rice and put it in a pan.
  4. Add all the veggies and add the spices.
  5. Crack the eggs and stir the rice until you can't see the eggs.
  6. Pour some soya sauce and stir the rice.
  7. Your precious fried rice is now ready! Enjoy it to your heart's content.
