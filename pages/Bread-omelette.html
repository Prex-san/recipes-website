Recipe for Bread Omelette


What is a bread omelette?

A bread omelette is an appetizing dish for breakfast or an evening snack.
It takes some time to prepare this scrumptious snack, but it is well worth the time.
A bread omelette is toasted and coated with eggs. It goes well with some tomato ketchup!

Ingredients

  • Bread(ofc)
  • Eggs(obviously)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Tomato Ketchup(optional)

Algorithm

To obtain the best bread omelette, follow these steps diligently
  1. Get a bowl and crack 2-4 eggs inside it. The number of eggs depends on your appetite.
  2. Add salt and pepper to the yolk. Stir the yolk until the color becomes uniform.
  3. Toast a few breads and immerse the toasted bread into the yolk.
  4. Toast the bread on a pan again after dipping it in the yolk in low flame.
  5. Wait until the yolk outside is cooked.
  6. Voila! Your first bread omelette is ready.
  7. Repeat the process until you obtain enough bread omelttes to satisfy your hunger.
